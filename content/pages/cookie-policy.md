## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and can provide anonymous usage information.

## Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies:** These are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They handle basic functions like page navigation and security.

**Analytics Cookies:** If analytics are enabled, we may use cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

## Third-Party Cookies

This site may embed content from YouTube or other platforms. These third-party services may set their own cookies. We do not control third-party cookies and recommend reviewing their respective cookie policies.

## Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on some websites, though TimesharePromo.com is designed to function without them.

Most browsers allow you to:
- View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
- Block third-party cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies
- Delete all cookies when you close your browser
