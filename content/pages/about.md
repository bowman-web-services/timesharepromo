## Who We Are

TimesharePromo.com is operated by [Bowman Web Services LLC](https://bowmanwebservices.com), a web services and digital marketing company based in Port Orange, Florida. We've been involved in the vacation club and resort promotion industry for over 15 years, and we believe the biggest problem in this space isn't the product — it's the lack of honest information.

## Why This Site Exists

Most timeshare content online falls into two categories: sales pages trying to get you to book immediately, or complaint sites trying to scare you away from the entire industry. Neither serves the traveler who simply wants to understand how timeshare promotions work, what the real savings look like, and how to find legitimate deals.

We built TimesharePromo.com to fill that gap with articles written from real industry experience — not from a marketing playbook.

## Our Position on Transparency

Every vacation club is, legally, a form of timeshare. We don't hide from that word, and we don't think the industry should either. The modern vacation club product has evolved significantly from the timeshares of the 1980s and 1990s, but consumers deserve to know what they're looking at.

We will never sell you an open-dated package. We will never misrepresent qualification requirements. We will never pressure you into a decision. If a promotion isn't right for you, we'll say so.
