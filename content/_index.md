## The Straight-Talk Timeshare Resource

Most timeshare websites fall into two camps: sites trying to sell you something, or sites telling you to run away. Neither is useful if you're genuinely trying to understand how timeshare promotions work and whether they're worth your time.

**We're the third option.** This site exists to give you clear, honest information about timeshare promotions, vacation club memberships, and all-inclusive resort deals — written by someone who's spent 15 years in the industry and believes transparency is the only way to operate.

### A Note on Honesty

Every vacation club is, legally, a form of timeshare. Some people in this industry go to great lengths to avoid that word. We don't. We use "vacation club" because that's how modern programs are structured — points-based, flexible, multi-resort — but we will never pretend the timeshare label doesn't apply.

If a program isn't right for you, we'll say so. If the savings aren't real, we'll tell you. This site is operated by [Bowman Web Services LLC](https://bowmanwebservices.com), and we put our name on everything we publish.
